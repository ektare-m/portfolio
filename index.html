Mallika Ektare | Frontend Developer | Charlotte, NC

After graduating from University of Colorado at Boulder, I landed my first job at Dot Hill Systems as a web developer. Dot Hill was eventually acquired by Seagate Technology, and I continued my work under Systems group and Lyve Pilot. In those 10 years, I've gained knowledge about the data industry, revamped an outdated user interface, gained a passion for user experience, and became a team lead. I look forward to expand my coding skills to include more backend development, as I am most interested in becoming a full stack developer.

I am now located in Charlotte, enjoying warmer weather and larger diversity. I look forward to connecting and networking with people in the software industry.
